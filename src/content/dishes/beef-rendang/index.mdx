Nasi Lemak is the national dish of Malaysia. It consists of a miminum of four rather humble components; coconut milk pandan rice, sambal A.K.A fried chili condiment, fried peanuts and anchovy, plus a cucumber and egg garnish. Add a piece of fried chicken and you have Nasi Lemak Ayam Goreng. Add some rendang and you're on the next level. Combining the food of the gods as I'm sure they intended. But it all starts from the humble rice, anchovy, sambal, egg, cucumber base.

In the same way that a new prisoner establishes themself in jail by walking in and picking a fight with the toughest looking bird, the foreigner learning to cook Malaysian food equivilent is walking into the kitchen and making Nasi Lemak.
